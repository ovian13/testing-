## The Mission

Tamil Nadu has 1,277 engineering colleges.
Fewer than 30 get campus recruitment.
1,247 colleges — invisible.

Not because the students lack skill.
Because no credible signal exists to prove they have it.

**Eakalaiva is that signal.**

> *If you cannot change the college you came from, change what you can prove.*
> **Prove. Grow. Rise.**

## The One Filter

Before any decision, any feature, any argument:

> **Does this close the gap or widen it?**

If you cannot answer in one sentence — the decision is not ready.

---

## The Ten Non-Negotiables

1. Students pay nothing. Ever. No exceptions.
2. All timestamps server-side UTC. Client time never trusted.
3. No XP without a cleared verification gate.
4. No credentials hardcoded. `.env` always.
5. No merge without one person reading the code.
6. Vimal writes every brief before every build session.
7. Ramadass gets at least one code review every week.
8. Ovian never absorbs tasks nobody else claimed.
9. Every task has exactly one name on it.
10. Equity and salary conversation before Week 4.

## The Conversation That Must Happen Before Week 4

Ovian raised financial sustainability on his intake form.
A co-founder wondering about money in month one has divided attention.

**Have this conversation explicitly. Document it in `logs/EQUITY.md`:**
- What is the equity split?
- Is there a stipend plan, and from what milestone?
- What does each person need to feel stable enough to give full output?

## What We Measure

| Person | Activity (wrong) | Outcome (right) |
|---|---|---|
| Vimal | "I wrote the product doc" | Ramadass can build from it without asking more than 2 questions |
| Ramadass | "I coded for 6 hours" | Feature is functional, committed, one teammate tested it |
| Ovian | "I messaged 10 colleges" | 2 colleges replied, 1 agreed to a call |

**A task is done when:**
the output exists, one other person tested it, it is committed, and the next person can act on it without asking what it does.
