## The One Filter
Every decision, feature, task, or debate ends here:

> **Does this close the gap or widen it?**

If you cannot answer in one sentence — the decision is not ready.

## The Ten Non-Negotiables

1. Students pay nothing. Ever. For anything. No exceptions.
2. All timestamps server-side UTC. Client time never trusted. Ever.
3. No XP without a cleared verification gate.
4. No credentials hardcoded. `.env` from hour one.
5. No merge without one person reading the code.
6. No vague commit messages. Ever.
7. Vimal is never assigned Discord moderation. Not once.
8. Ramadass never codes alone without a feedback checkpoint that week.
9. Ovian never absorbs overflow tasks that nobody else claimed.
10. Equity and salary conversation happens before the project is 4 weeks old.

## Weekly Operating Rhythm

| Day | What Happens | Who |
|---|---|---|
| **Monday** | Planning — 3 tasks per person written on shared board. Vimal posts the week's product brief. | All three |
| **Wednesday** | Async check-in — each person posts one sentence update in team chat: what shipped, what's blocked | All three |
| **Friday** | Weekly review — what shipped, what didn't, risk watchlist checked (see below) | All three |
| **Sunday** | Optional — Vimal writes next week's product brief if complex features are coming | Vimal |

**Rule:** If Friday review is skipped, Monday planning is not valid until it happens.

## How We Measure Outcomes (Not Activity)

| Founder | Activity (wrong) | Outcome (right) |
|---|---|---|
| **Vimal** | "I wrote the product doc" | Decision is documented AND Ramadass can build from it without asking more than 2 questions |
| **Ramadass** | "I coded for 6 hours" | Feature is functional, committed to dev branch, one teammate has tested it |
| **Ovian** | "I messaged 10 colleges" | 2 colleges replied, 1 agreed to a call |

**A task is done when:**
1. The output exists and is visible
2. One person other than the builder has tested or reviewed it
3. It is committed with a clear message
4. The next person in chain can act on it without asking what it does

## The Risk Watchlist (Check Every Friday)

- [ ] Is Vimal on product thinking — not managing tasks?
- [ ] Did Ramadass get at least one code review this week?
- [ ] Is Ovian's task count equal to Ramadass's visible task count?
- [ ] Has the equity/salary conversation happened? (Must be done by Week 4)
- [ ] Is the task board up to date with exactly one owner per task?
- [ ] Is any founder showing signs of quiet disengagement?

**If any box is unchecked two Fridays in a row — it becomes the first item on Monday planning. Not the last.**

## The Conversation That Must Happen Before Week 4

Ovian raised financial sustainability on his intake form. This is not a small thing.
A co-founder who is worried about money in month one is a co-founder with divided attention.

**Have this conversation explicitly:**
- What is the equity split?
- Is there any stipend plan, and if so, from what milestone?
- What does each person need to feel financially stable enough to give full output?

Document the outcome in `DECISION_LOG.md`. Do not leave it as a verbal agreement.
